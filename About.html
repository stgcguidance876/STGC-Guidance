Welcome to the About us page:

St. Georges College

St. George's College is an all-male,
Roman Catholic high school in Kingston,
Jamaica founded in 1850 by 21 Spanish
Jesuits who had been exiled from
Colombia as part of a religious persecution.
Although it is an all-male institution it
has a Pre-University Programme (Sixth Form)
to female students

Website Profile

My website is about helping students
in very dreadful circumstances who are
having issues attending online classes
due to a shortage of devices. The devices
they can be requested vary from 10-inch
Tablets to laptops.
